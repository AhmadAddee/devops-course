## Description
The paper "Understanding and Detecting Peer Dependency Resolving Loop in npm Ecosystem" introduces PeerSpin, a previously overlooked problem in the npm ecosystem, where peer dependency conflicts can lead to infinite loops during dependency resolution.  
The authors analyze the root causes of PeerSpin and propose a novel detection technique implemented in the tool PeerChecker.  
The paper’s goals are mainly two: the first one is to provide the first systematic understanding of PeerSpin, and the second one is to offer practical tools and insights to detect and mitigate it.

- *Link to article*: (https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11029857)

Relevance
The paper is relevant to DevOps and DevSecOps as it highlights critical risks in dependency management within CI/CD pipelines.
